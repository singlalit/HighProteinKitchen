Crispy Soya Besan Tikki

Ingredients:

  • 50g Soya Chunks (boiled & lightly ground)
  • 50g Besan (Gram Flour)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 Green Chilli (finely chopped)
  • Fresh Coriander (chopped)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Red Chilli Powder (as per taste)
  • 1-2 tsp Oil (for shallow frying)

Instructions:

  1. Boil soya chunks, squeeze out excess water, and grind for 3-5 seconds.
  2. Mix soya chunks with besan, onion, tomato, green chilli, coriander, salt, and red chilli powder.
  3. Shape the mixture into tikkis or patties.
  4. Heat a pan with a little oil and shallow fry the tikkis for 3-4 minutes on medium-low heat until golden brown.
  5. Flip and cook the other side until crispy and fully cooked.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney or yogurt dip.
