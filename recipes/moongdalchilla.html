Sprouted Moong Dal Chilla

Ingredients:

  • Sprouted moong dal: 50g (‚Çπ20)
  • Besan (Gram flour): 20g (‚Çπ3)
  • Onion: 1 small (‚Çπ3)
  • Tomato: 1 small (‚Çπ3)
  • Green chilies: 2 (‚Çπ2)
  • Coriander leaves: Handful (‚Çπ2)
  • Spices: (Turmeric, Red Chili, Garam Masala) - (‚Çπ5)
  • Salt: To taste
  • Oil: 1 tbsp (‚Çπ5)
  • Total Cost: ~ ‚Çπ43

Instructions:

  1. Blend sprouted moong dal with little water into a smooth batter.
  2. Finely chop onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and coriander leaves.
  3. Mix besan and chopped veggies into the batter, adding spices and salt.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan, pour batter, and spread into a thin chilla.
  5. Cook on medium flame until golden brown, flipping once.
  6. Serve hot with green chutney or yogurt.
