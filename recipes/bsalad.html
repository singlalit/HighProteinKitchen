Refreshing Beetroot Salad with Curd & Tadka

Looking for a quick, refreshing, and highly nutritious salad? This Beetroot Salad with Curd & Tadka is a perfect blend of earthy sweetness from beetroots, creamy curd, and flavorful Indian spices.

Ingredients:

  • For the Salad:
  • 2 medium-sized beetroots (boiled and grated)
  • 1 bowl of curd (yogurt)
  • A pinch of chaat masala
  • A pinch of black pepper
  • A pinch of salt
  • Fresh coriander (chopped, for garnish)

  • For the Tadka (Tempering):
  • 1 teaspoon oil (olive oil, ghee, or mustard oil)
  • ½ teaspoon black mustard seeds
  • ½ teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of hing (asafoetida)
  • 4-5 curry leaves

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Prepare the Beetroot Salad
    • Boil the beetroots until soft (about 10-15 minutes), then let them cool.
    • Grate the beetroots into a bowl.
    • Add curd, chaat masala, black pepper, and salt. Mix well until everything is combined.
  2. Step 2: Prepare the Tadka (Tempering)
    • Heat 1 teaspoon of oil in a small pan.
    • Add mustard seeds and let them pop.
    • Add cumin seeds, hing, and curry leaves, and sauté for a few seconds.
    • Turn off the heat and pour this tempering over the beetroot salad.
  3. Step 3: Garnish & Serve
    • Mix everything gently and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
    • Serve chilled or at room temperature as a side dish or a light meal.
